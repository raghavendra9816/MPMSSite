Our Story

A school built around the child, not the syllabus.

Mother's Pride Montessori School was founded on a simple belief: every child is naturally curious, capable, and eager to learn — when given the right environment. On Gramin Road in the heart of Lahan, we've built classrooms where children move freely, choose purposeful work, and learn at their own pace, guided by trained Montessori educators.

We combine the discipline of the Montessori method — practical life, sensorial exploration, language, and mathematics — with genuine care for each family we serve. Small class sizes, real Montessori materials, and a calm, orderly environment help children build independence, focus, and a lifelong love of learning.

"Follow the child, and they will show you what they need to grow."
The Montessori Method

Three principles guide every classroom.

Practical Life

Children practice real, purposeful tasks — pouring, buttoning, cleaning, caring for plants — building independence, coordination, and concentration from the very first day.

Sensorial Learning

Hands-on Montessori materials refine each sense — sight, sound, touch, and weight — giving children the vocabulary and precision to make sense of the world around them.

Language & Numeracy

Through phonetic sounds, movable letters, and concrete number materials, children build a natural, joyful bridge from concrete experience to reading, writing, and math.

Programs by Age

A learning path that grows with your child.

Stage One

Toddler Community

18 months – 3 years

A gentle, secure first step outside home — building language, movement, and early independence in a prepared, child-sized environment.

Stage Two

Primary (Casa)

3 – 6 years

The core Montessori classroom — practical life, sensorial materials, early literacy and numeracy, in mixed-age groups that teach as much as they learn.

Stage Three

Lower Elementary

6 – 9 years

Curiosity expands into research, storytelling, and collaborative projects, as children move from concrete materials toward abstract reasoning.
